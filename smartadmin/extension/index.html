How It Works

1

Install the Extension

Add SmartAdmin Login Assistant to your Chrome browser from the Chrome Web Store.

2

Save Your Credentials

Log in to SmartAdmin through your browser. The extension will offer to save your credentials and TOTP secret.

3

Configure Your Preferences

Open the extension popup to adjust settings like auto-login, credential retention, and cleanup policies.

4

Launch Desktop App

Click the "Login to SmartAdmin App" button to send credentials to your desktop application via deep-link.
