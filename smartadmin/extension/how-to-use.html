How to Use

Follow these simple steps to set up and use the SmartAdmin Login Assistant extension for seamless browser-to-app authentication.

1

Install the Extension

First, add the SmartAdmin Login Assistant to your Chrome or Firefox browser. Click the "Install Extension" button and follow the installation process.

2

Pin the Extension

After installation, pin the extension to your Chrome toolbar for easy access. Click the puzzle piece icon in your browser toolbar and pin SmartAdmin Login Assistant.

3

First-Time Setup

Click on the extension icon to open the popup. You'll see the initial setup screen where you can configure your preferences and enable features like auto-save and TOTP generation.

4

Login to SmartAdmin

Navigate to your SmartAdmin login page and enter your credentials. The extension will detect the login form and offer to save your username and password for future use.

5

Launch Desktop App

Make sure the SmartAdmin App is running.
Click the "Login to SmartAdmin App" button in the extension popup to send your credentials directly to the desktop application via deep-link protocol.
